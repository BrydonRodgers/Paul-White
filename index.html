What We Repair

All your appliances,
expertly fixed

Our certified technicians handle every major brand and appliance type — right at your home, with parts in the van.

01
Refrigerators & Freezers

Not cooling, making noise, or icing over? We fix compressors, thermostats, ice makers, and door seals on all major brands.

02
Washing Machines

Leaking, vibrating, or stopping mid-cycle? We handle drums, motors, pumps, and electronic control boards.

03
Dryers

Clothes still damp? Dryer won't start? We replace heating elements, belts, sensors, and thermostats on the spot.

04
Dishwashers

Not cleaning or won't drain? We fix spray arms, pumps, water valves, door latches, and control boards.

05
Ovens & Stoves

Won't heat up or burns unevenly? We repair igniters, elements, burners, and oven controls for gas and electric.

06
Microwaves & Range Hoods

Not heating or sparking? We service countertop and built-in microwaves and range hood fans of all brands.
